BrightSign Bootstrap

Use this page to generate an autorun.brs that you can download to an SD card, put in your BrightSign player and Go!

 Run From My Website

This is the easiest option, but if the network connection slows down or disappears, your sign will stop working.

 Run From My BrightSign Player

This option requires creating a manifest, but if the network connection slows down or disappears, your sign will continue to work. Once you enter the URL of your manifest, and you have installed the autorun.brs on your BrightSign player, the player will download all the assets mentioned in the manifest, then start playing index.html
